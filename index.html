About Us

Discover Your Paradise by the Sea

Since 2008, West Bay Palm Tree Beach Resort has served as a sanctuary where every stay is a journey into peace and beauty. Founded by visionary hotelier Ajith Kumar, whose portfolio includes several prestigious 3 and 4-star properties, the resort is the crown jewel of a commitment to hospitality excellence.
Perched on the pristine shores of Odayam Beach, Varkala, we offer an exclusive coastal experience featuring private beach access, stunning sea-view rooms, and the undeniable charm of beachside dining. Whether you are seeking rejuvenation through Yoga and Ayurveda, or creating memories with candlelight dinners and vibrant celebrations, your perfect escape awaits under the palms.

The Vision of Our Founder
Under the leadership of Chairman Ajith Kumar, the mission extends beyond luxury to redefining the Kerala experience. His vision is built on bringing world-class standards and international-grade facilities to the heart of God's Own Country. By integrating sophisticated infrastructure with local heritage, he strives to promote Kerala tourism globally, ensuring the region's soulful beauty meets the highest world-class expectations.

Activities

Our diverse range of activities is designed to offer something for everyone.
Restaurant

Delicious Food Awaken Your Taste Bugs


Experience the essence of coastal living at our premier restaurant. Set against the soothing sound of the waves, we blend traditional Kerala spices with global favorites. From tropical drinks to exquisite meals, we provide a sanctuary of flavor where guests relax and create unforgettable memories over the finest dining in Varkala.

Beachside Dining Experience


Savor the freshest catch of the day right on the shores of Odayam Beach. Enjoy stunning ocean views and the sound of gentle waves as you dine, creating a truly magical culinary experience.
